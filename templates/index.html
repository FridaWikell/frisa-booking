Member benefits

Embark on a crafting journey that knows no bounds! As a privileged member of our cosmic crafting community, you've unlocked the portal to endless creativity.

For our future members (a.k.a. browsers & dreamers)

  • Inspiration gathering- Just peeking through the keyhole of creativity? Welcome! Wander through our workshops for a sprinkle of inspiration. When you're ready to dive into the world of unlimited crafting, our doors are always open for new members. Until then, browse, dream, and be inspired. Who knows? You might just find a workshop that tickles your fancy more than goat yoga does!
  • Book Workshops - Leap into action and secure your spot in our much-coveted workshops. From the complexities of constructing with Lego to the artistry of upcycling old cassette tapes, your next adventure awaits!
  • Edit Bookings - Changed your mind? Prefer perler beads to upcycling? Swap your workshops faster than you can say “Decoupage!” Flexibility is the heart of creativity, after all.
  • Cancel Bookings - Clash with your goat yoga session? No worries! Cancel your booking with the click of a button. Your zen and your crafts should never be in conflict.
  • View Your Booking - Ever find yourself pondering, "Did I sign up for perler beads or transforming treasures?" Fret not! Your bookings are neatly listed for a quick reminder. Never miss a beat or a bead!
